Our Lessons

Our lessons aim to encourage greater focus and cognitive function. We have experienced teacher who are passionate about music and will make the effort to understand how each student learns. We teach a range of string, wind and brass instruments. The Rangataua Forest location even has a piano situated in the forest for piano lessons.
