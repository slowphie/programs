Welcome to Silvereye Acoustics

Here at Silvereye Acoustics we offer a learning environment, in the environment. Surrounded by nature, we believe our students learn the best in a calm peaceful setting to let their creativity flow. Not only is this beneficial for their learning but it is stress and negativity reducing, and increases the sense of well being through this experience.

We started this business based off immersing ourselves in the great outdoors and disconnecting from technology. We teach acoustic instruments therefore have no need for electronic gear. Study were done to prove spending time in nature improves creativity and focus, due to the decreased stimulations and distractions of modern life. Nature improves cognitive function and memory, two key aspects when learning an instrument. This is also why we not only teach in these locations but offer spaces to hire for practicing or performing. If you are looking to experience the tranquility New Zealand nature has to offer, we highly recommend trying us out. With no obligation to a fixed plan of lessons, you can book a lesson with the intention of merely escaping for a one-off experience.
