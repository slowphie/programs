Our Locations...

Silvereye Acoustics

Office Studio

Our office studio is located at the end of Mangateitei Road. There is where you will meet your teacher before the lesson and before you set off to your location. Some of of locations are just a short walk from our office building, others require a trip from our company 4WD to get there.

254-178 Mangateitei Rd, Rangataua 4691

Lake Rotokura

Lake Rotokura is a short drive up the Forestry Road into the forest. The site is on the edge of the lake. just beyond the trees looking out across the water. There is a clear view of Mount Ruapehu in the distance.

Rangataua Forest

Immerse yourself in the New Zealand native trees and birds in the Rangataua Forest. This location also has a piano situated amoungst the trees, and the sound of birds to accompany your beautiful playing.

Mangaehuehu Clearing

Mangaehuehu clearing is found via a 5 minute walk from the office studio. The local farmer occassionally has stock at this location. They are inquisitive and love to listen to the music.
